# Assignment-2
A comprehensive business analytics glossary demonstrating understanding of data concepts, KPIs, and real-world applicatio
In this assignment, we were asked to research and explain 38 essential business analytics concepts. For each term, we wrote a clear definition in our own words, provided a real-world example, and cited credible sources. We also had to submit everything by hand, ensuring the examples we gave were practical and easy to understand. This helped us build a strong vocabulary and apply these ideas to real business situations.
